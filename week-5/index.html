🧪 Week 5 – Guided Practice (List Wizard Training)

Open your browser console (Ctrl/⌘ + Shift + J) to cast your spells.

📜 Your Spellbook (Inventory)

Try in the console: addItem('🧪 Potion of Wisdom'), removeItem(0), replaceItem(0, 'Phoenix Feather')

🧙‍♀️ Notes

  • addItem uses push() to add to the array and then updates the DOM.
  • removeItem(index) uses splice() to delete by position.
  • replaceItem(index, newItem) swaps an existing item (via assignment or splice()).
  • displayItems() rebuilds the list inside <span id="items">.
